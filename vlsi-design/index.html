VLSI Physical Design Flow

This project showcases my experience with RTL-to-GDSII flow for a sample SoC block. The focus is on:

  • Logic synthesis (Design Compiler)
  • Placement and routing (Innovus)
  • Timing closure and signoff (PrimeTime)
  • Power integrity and IR drop analysis

Tools used: Synopsys Design Compiler, Cadence Innovus, Synopsys PrimeTime.

Highlights

Implemented dynamic power optimization techniques, clock gating, and multi-voltage domains. Achieved a 20% area reduction compared to baseline while meeting strict timing constraints.
